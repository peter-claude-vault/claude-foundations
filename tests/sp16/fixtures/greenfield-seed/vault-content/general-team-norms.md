# Team Norms

General operating norms for the Aria Strategy Group team. Applies across all engagements (Alpha, Beta, and future workstreams).

## Communication

- Default to async written updates in the engagement workspace.
- Steering committee decisions captured as meeting notes within 24 hours.
- Quarterly executive readouts delivered as a single consolidated brief.

## Cadence defaults

- Workstream sync: weekly.
- Steering committee: weekly during active phases; biweekly during steady-state.
- Quarterly executive readout: end of each quarter.
