# Beta Launch — Rollout Sequence

Beta enterprise rollout launch readiness gating pilot windows. Detailed sequencing for the six-week phased deployment.

## Sequence

1. Week 1: enterprise pilot bootstrap; first-segment cohort onboarded.
2. Week 2: gating decision on second-segment expansion.
3. Week 3: second-segment cohort onboarded; readiness review.
4. Week 4: third-segment expansion gating.
5. Week 5: third-segment cohort onboarded; full enterprise rollout.
6. Week 6: post-rollout retrospective + executive readout.

## Risks

- Gating decision latency on segments two and three.
- Pilot windows may slip if readiness gates fail.
